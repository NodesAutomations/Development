### Usefull Shortcuts
- Playback speed : `Shift` + `.` or `,`
- Mute Toggle : `M`
- Full Screen Toggle : `F`
- Caption Toggle : `C`
- Volume : `Up Arrow` + `Down Arrow`
- Restart Video : `0`
- Jump to specific Percentage :  `1-9` for 10% to 90%
- Skip to next Chapter : `Ctrl` + `Left Arrow` or `Right Arrow`
